About

Music Queue is a client side web player for your music stored in Google Drive. The only purpose of MQ is to provide the private access to your music resources stored in the Google Drive account.

Features:
  • Tree navigation - a handy way to find the albums and songs you want to listen to
  • Customizable playlist - combine the full albums or individual songs in the desired order
  • Drag and drop - simply drag the files or folders onto your playlist to enqueue them
  • Access to the shared files - listen to the music shared with you by others
  • Basic shortcuts - keyboard shortcuts to control basic functions
  • No flash required - pure HTML and Javascript

Browser support

MQ is powered by HTML5 <audio> tag which means you'll need one of the modern browsers that support HTML5:

Internet Explorer * Chrome Opera Safari ** Firefox Android iOS
HTML5 Support 9.0+ 3.0+ 10.5+ 3.1+ 3.5+ 2.2+ 3.0+
MQ tested 9.0+ 35.0 29.0+

*Internet Explorer users must add an exception to the Privacy settings to allow using cookies for the "google.com" domain: 1) Go to Tools->Internet Options->Privacy->Sites 2) Enter "google.com" and press "Allow" button.

**Safari users must have Quick Time installed to enable HTML5 <audio> feature.

Please, also note, to use MQ the Javascript must be enabled in your browser.
